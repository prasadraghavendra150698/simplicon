# SEO Setup for Google Visibility

## What's Already Done

- **robots.txt** – Allows all crawlers, points to sitemap
- **sitemap.xml** – Lists all pages with lastmod, changefreq, priority
- **Meta tags** – Unique title, description, keywords on each page
- **Canonical URLs** – Each page has correct canonical
- **Open Graph & Twitter Card** – For social sharing
- **JSON-LD** – Organization, LocalBusiness, BreadcrumbList, WebSite
- **robots meta** – `index, follow, max-image-preview:large, max-snippet:-1, max-video-preview:-1`

## Steps to Get Indexed in Google

1. **Submit to Google Search Console**
   - Go to [search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
   - Add property: `https://simplicontax.com`
   - Verify ownership (HTML meta tag or DNS)
   - Submit sitemap: `https://simplicontax.com/sitemap.xml`

2. **Request Indexing**
   - In Search Console → URL Inspection → enter your homepage
   - Click "Request Indexing" for key pages

3. **Build Backlinks**
   - Get listed in directories (tax, business, India)
   - Share on social media
   - Guest posts or mentions on relevant sites

4. **Keep Content Fresh**
   - Update sitemap lastmod when you change pages
   - Add new content (blog, resources) over time
